About Hemanth

Analytics rigor, automation mindset, and clear business storytelling.

Hemanth Kumar Konda is a results-oriented Data Analyst with an M.S. in Informatics from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. His work sits at the intersection of data analysis, business intelligence, automation, and applied AI, with hands-on experience across Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, SAP S/4HANA, and workflow tools.

He focuses on turning raw, scattered, or operational data into structured insights that teams can actually use. That means building KPI dashboards, cleaning and preparing datasets, automating repetitive reporting steps, and translating technical findings into business decisions.

His approach is simple: make data easier to trust, easier to explore, and easier to act on. Whether the outcome is a dashboard, an ETL workflow, an AI-powered assistant, or a forecasting model, the goal stays the same: measurable impact and sharper decisions.
